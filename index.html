Where It Begins

Your lovely cottage on the edge of the forest is a mess. Chairs thrown about, jars broken, objects on the ground when they should be on the table and objects on the table that should be on the ground. Who did this? You had just arrived home from a relaxing day collecting berries and singing to the birds, how could this be? A note, stabbed into the doorframe by an elaborate knife, catches your eye:

What you hold most precious to you is now mine. If you want it back, you'll have to fight me for it. You have three days. I will meet you in the crypts beneath the castle beyond the woods.


"What the heck!" you shout. Obviously, you would accept this challenge.
